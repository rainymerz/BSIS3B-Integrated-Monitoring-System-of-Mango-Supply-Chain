Welcome to Mangrowth


Where innovation meets agriculture to monitor the journey of mangoes from farm to table.
We are passionate team that is committed to redefine the mango supply chain,
ensuring that every mango crop you eat is a great evidence to quality and sustainability agriculture.

Mission


At Mangrowth, our mission is to revolutionize innovation and promote sustainability
for mango crops under the area of agriculture. The integration of monitoring
technologies into every stages of mango crop journey , ensuring the production,
harvest, manufacturing and delivery processes are optimized for maximum efficiency
and have great impact on the business. By fostering sustainability practices,
we aim to deliver better quality but an unparalleled experience - from farm to table.

Vision


Our vision is to enrich the lives of the consumers promoting seamless processes and
sustainability practices for mango crops. We envision an integrated monitoring system,
leveraging technologies to formulate transparent and efficient supply chain. Mangrowth
aspires to be the catalyst for change in mango supply chain, where every mango is a
testament to better quality and promote sustainability agriculture.
